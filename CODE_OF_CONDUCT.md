# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone who participates in the Lotto Lens project.

We pledge to make participation in our community a respectful and positive experience for all, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Educational Focus

This project is for educational purposes only. All contributors must:

- Maintain the educational nature of the app
- Not promote gambling or make prediction claims
- Keep disclaimers intact and prominent
- Respect the privacy-first approach

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

1. Opening an issue (for public matters)
2. Contacting the project maintainer directly via GitHub

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

### Consequences

Project maintainers may take action they deem appropriate, including:

- Warning the offender
- Temporary ban from the project
- Permanent ban from the project

### Enforcement Guidelines

**1. Correction**
- **Action**: Private written warning
- **Consequence**: Explanation of violation and apology may be requested

**2. Warning**
- **Action**: Warning with consequences for continued behavior
- **Consequence**: Temporary interaction restrictions

**3. Temporary Ban**
- **Action**: Temporary ban from any interaction
- **Consequence**: No public or private interaction for specified period

**4. Permanent Ban**
- **Action**: Permanent ban from project
- **Consequence**: No further interaction permitted

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies:
