## Overview

Lucky Stats ("the app", "we", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

## Information We Collect

### Data We DO NOT Collect

Lucky Stats does **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

- ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Contact information
- ❌ Payment information
- ❌ Advertising identifiers
- ❌ Analytics or tracking data
- ❌ User accounts or passwords
- ❌ Social media information
- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ Browsing history

### Data We Access

**Public Lottery Data:**
- The app downloads publicly available Mega Millions drawing results from the New York State Open Data portal (data.ny.gov)
- This data is already publicly available and contains no personal information
- The data includes: drawing dates, winning numbers, and bonus numbers

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

**Cached Lottery Data:**
- Historical lottery drawing results are stored on your device for offline use
- This data is public lottery information only
- Stored using Apple's standard UserDefaults and local file system

**User Preferences:**
- Your app settings and preferences (theme, selected date ranges, etc.)
- Stored locally using Apple's UserDefaults
- Never transmitted off your device

## How We Use Information

**Lottery Data:**
- Downloaded from public data sources
- Cached locally for offline functionality
- Used to display historical trends and statistics
- Never shared or transmitted to third parties

**User Preferences:**
- Stored to remember your settings
- Used only to personalize your app experience
- Never leaves your device

## Third-Party Services

**Data Sources:**
- **data.ny.gov** - New York State Open Data Portal
  - Purpose: Download public lottery drawing results
  - Data Transmitted: API requests for public data only
  - Privacy Policy: https://data.ny.gov/privacy-policy

**No Other Third Parties:**
- No analytics services
- No advertising networks
- No crash reporting
- No social media integration

## Data Security

**Local Storage:**
- All data is stored locally on your device
- Protected by iOS security mechanisms
- We do not have access to your device data

**Network Security:**
- API requests use HTTPS encryption
- Only connects to data.ny.gov for public data
- No transmission of personal information

## Data Retention

**Cached Data:**
- Lottery data remains on your device until you delete it
- You can clear cached data anytime using the "Clear All Data" option in the Data tab
- Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

**User Preferences:**
- Settings remain until you delete the app
- No data persists after app deletion

## Your Rights and Choices

**Access and Deletion:**
- All data is stored locally on your device
- You have complete control over all stored data
- Delete all data by:
  1. Using "Clear All Data" in the app's Data tab, OR
  2. Deleting the app from your device

**Opt-Out:**
- No data collection requires opt-out
- All features work without data sharing

## Children's Privacy

- This app is not intended for users under 18 years of age
- We do not knowingly collect information from minors
- If you are under 18, do not use this app
- Parents: If your child has used this app, simply delete it to remove all data
